Data Release

FloVerse Dataset

FloVerse consists of two complementary components: richly annotated floor plan maps and paired expert navigation trajectories, together enabling comprehensive training and evaluation of indoor navigation agents.

Part I
Floor Plan Maps

High-resolution floor plan images with semantic room-type annotations, wall topology graphs, and scale-normalized coordinate systems. Covers residential, commercial, and mixed-use layouts across diverse architectural styles.

Semantic Segmentation Room Topology Scale-Normalized Multi-Resolution
10K+ Floor Plans
50+ Scene Types
8 Room Categories
Download (HuggingFace) Data Card
Part II
Expert Trajectories

Human expert navigation demonstrations recorded in simulation, aligned to corresponding floor plan coordinates. Each trajectory includes step-level action labels, egocentric observations, and goal annotations.

Data Construction & Statistics

Overview of the data curation pipeline and key statistical properties of the FloVerse dataset across scene types and trajectory distributions.

[ Data construction pipeline figure ]

Figure 1. FloVerse data construction pipeline. Floor plans are collected, semantically annotated, and aligned with simulation environments. Expert trajectories are then recorded by human operators and post-processed to remove noise.

Method

Model Pipeline

Our approach encodes the floor plan as a structured prior and fuses it with egocentric perception to generate spatially grounded navigation plans.

[ Model pipeline figure ]

Figure 2. Overview of the proposed architecture. A floor plan encoder extracts topological and semantic features, which are cross-attended with the visual observation stream to produce waypoint predictions and action distributions.
